About Me

Hi there! My name is Jessica Au and I would like to welcome to my home page.

I received my B.S from UC Davis in 2017 in Mathematics and Scientific Computation, with an emphasis in Biology. Because of my interest in Genetics and Genomics, I obtained a minor in Bioinformatics and Quantitative Biology

I define myself as a computational mathematician who enjoys delving into the complexities of biological problems. Using my mathematical reasoning and computer programming, I specialize in simplifying biological questions to make them more tangible.
