About Me

Greetings!

My name is Jessica Au and welcome to my home page.

I define myself as a computational mathematician who enjoys delving into the complexities of biological problems. Using my mathematical reasoning and computer programming, I specialize in simplifying biological questions in order to make them more tangible.

I received my B.S from University of California- Davis in 2017 in Mathematics and Scientific Computation, with an emphasis in Biology. I have a great interest in Genetics and Genomics, which led me to pursue a minor in Bioinformatics and Quantitative Biology

My coursework comprised of courses in computer science [object oriented programming and data structures], statistics or probability theory, as well as different biology courses [gene and gene expression, biochemistry, and functional genomics].
