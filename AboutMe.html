About Me

My name is Jessica N. Au and I was born and raised in San Jose, California. I made this website in order to personalize the way others could discover more about me and my work. This is still a work in progress- hence the title of my webpage, "Jess in Progress". I don't know for sure what I would like to have on here yet, so I hope you come back to visit periodically for new updates.
Education
In regards to my education, I graduated with a B.S in Mathematics and Scientific Computation, with a biology emphasis from University of California- Davis. I have a great interest in genetics and genomics, which led me to pursue a minor in Bioinformatics and Quantitative Biology.
Coursework & Knowledge
My coursework comprised of courses in computer science, statistics, biology, and mathematics. I have knowledge of object-oriented programming and data structures, touched upon probability theory and general statistics, and a multitude of biology courses such as gene expression, biochemistry, & functional genomics. My mathematics courses were more geared towards numerical analysis and rigorous use of other mathematical tools to investigate different biological/physical systems. From my lab experience, I am knowledgeable in fields such as population ecology, electrophysiology, and structural biology. If you're interested in my coursework, please go to the "Other" tab in the navigation bar to download my CV.
So who am I?
Professionally, I define myself as a computational mathematician who enjoys delving into the complexities of different types of problems. I am interested in using my mathematical reasoning and computer programming to simplify more extensive and large-scale problems in order to make them more tangible and easy to understand. I hope to be a part of a team that does not limit my abilities to learn, discover, and develop new innovative tools to help others. Personally, I like to relax during my off time or indulge in my many hobbies such as gardening, goldfish husbandry, weight-lifting, or play with my forever curious and treat tracking dog- Sparky.
