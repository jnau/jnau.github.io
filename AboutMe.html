About Me

My full name is Jessica N. Au. I was born and raised in San Jose, California. I made this website in order to personalize the way others could find out more about me and my work. It is still a work in progress- hence the title of my webpage, "Jess in Progress". I don't know for sure what I would like to have on here yet, so I hope you come back to visit periodically for new updates.
Education
I graduated with a B.S in Mathematics and Scientific Computation, with a biology emphasis from UC Davis. I have a great interest in Genetics and Genomics, which led me to pursue a minor in Bioinformatics and Quantitative Biology.
Coursework & Knowledge
My coursework comprised of courses in computer science, statistics, biology, and mathematics. I have knowledge of object-oriented programming and data structures, touched upon probability theory and general statistics, and a multitude of biology courses such as gene expression, biochemistry, & functional genomics. My mathematics courses were more geared towards numerical analysis and rigorous use of other mathematical tools to investigate different biological/physical systems.
What I hope to Acheive
I define myself as a computational mathematician who enjoys delving into the complexities of biological problems. I am interested in using my mathematical reasoning and computer programming to simplify biological questions in order to make them more tangible and easy to understand. I hope to be a part of a team that does not limit my abilities to learn, discover, and develop new innovative tools to help others.
