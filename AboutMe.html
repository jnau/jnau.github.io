About Me

My name is Jessica Au. I was born and raised in San Jose, California.
I graduated with a B.S in Mathematics and Scientific Computation, with a biology emphasis from UC Davis. I have a great interest in Genetics and Genomics, which led me to pursue a minor in Bioinformatics and Quantitative Biology
My coursework included computer science, statistics, and biology courses, along with my mathematics courses. To be more specific, I have knowledge of object oriented programming and data structures, touched on probability theory and general statistics, and a multitude of biology courses such as gene expression, biochemistry, functional genomics, and more.
I define myself as a computational mathematician who enjoys delving into the complexities of biological problems. Using my mathematical reasoning and computer programming, I specialize in simplifying biological questions in order to make them more tangible.
